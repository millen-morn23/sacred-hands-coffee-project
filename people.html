Our Philosophy

At Sacred Hands Coffee, we believe that great coffee is only possible through great relationships. Our network extends beyond traditional supply chains to create meaningful partnerships that benefit everyone involved from the smallholder farmers who nurture each coffee plant to the roasters who bring out the unique character of our beans.

Every person in our network contributes their expertise, passion, and dedication to ensure that Sacred Hands Coffee maintains its commitment to quality, sustainability, and positive impact.

Leadership Team

The visionaries behind Sacred Hands Coffee Project

Hansel Iravonga

Founder & Project Director

"My vision for Sacred Hands Coffee came from witnessing the untapped potential of Western Kenya's coffee farmers. I established this project to create direct pathways for smallholder farmers to access better markets while maintaining the quality and character that makes our region special."

With a background in agricultural development and a deep understanding of local farming practices, Hansel leads our mission to transform coffee cultivation in Western Kenya through education, quality focus, and sustainable practices.

Sarah Wanjiku

Head of Field Operations

"Working directly with farmers has taught me that sustainable change happens through consistent support and education. Our Farmer Field School program isn't just about teaching techniques,it's about building confidence and community."

Sarah coordinates our on-ground activities, manages relationships with farming communities, and ensures that our quality standards are maintained throughout the cultivation and processing phases.
