Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Sacred Hands Coffee

What are your minimum order quantities for wholesale?

Our minimum order for wholesale partners is typically 5 bags (150kg), but we're flexible based on your needs and can work with smaller roasters on custom arrangements.

How can I visit your farms and operations?

We welcome visitors and organize farm tours for partners and serious buyers. Please contact us at least 2 weeks in advance to arrange your visit to Western Kenya.

What certifications do you have?

We're currently working toward organic and fair trade certifications. All our coffee is grown using sustainable practices and we provide complete traceability for every lot.

How do you ensure quality consistency?

Through our Farmer Field School program, we provide year-round training on quality standards, selective harvesting, and proper processing techniques. Each lot is cupped and graded before export.
