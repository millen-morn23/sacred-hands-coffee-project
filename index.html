Our Mission

The Sacred Hands Coffee Project is transforming the coffee landscape in Western Kenya through producer-led initiatives that focus on quality, sustainability, and direct farmer relationships.

We assist smallholder farmers in organizing and acquiring knowledge about quality coffee cultivation, creating traceable micro-lots while improving livelihoods.
